By:   Sonali Sridhar  Twitter:  [@jollysonali](https://twitter.com/jollysonali?lang=en) 

####Social Rules  
* treat students like adults - build trust and respect
* don't want students to be fearful
* No feigning surprise - "oh, you don't now him? it?"
* "No, well actually" - don't derail a conversation to correct a detail
* No back seat driving - interrupting to offer a comment w/o engaging in a conversation.  Step into the conversation, introduce yourself; else catch someone at lunch.  
* No subtle-isms - small things that make people feel uncomfortable
* Don't pile on
