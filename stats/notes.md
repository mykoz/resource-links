
**Splitting the Data**  
If you can afford it, split the data 3 ways:
 * exploratory 
    * training (plot it, play, build models)
    * validation
    * (could later combine training + validation for info; open)
 * test

How to split
 * lots of data:  1/3, 1/3, 1/3
 * not too much data  
      * power  
      * sample size
